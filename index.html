Music Club

The Music club aims to nurture the talent in every member and provide a conducive environment for mutual learning. We believe that each individual has something to offer and through mentorships, jams, competitions and events we strive to polish skills, share knowledge and spread musical joy. If music is a way of life for you, the Som's Music Club provides opportunities to learn more about music, assist students wanting to transfer to a four-year college music program and offers activities and performance venues for students to share their interest in music. Students need only have an interest in music to join.

Drama Club

The Drama club aims to provide those with an initial interest in acting an opportunity to learn more about the art. To set up a drama club is to strengthen members’ skills of understanding and interpreting dramatic texts through reading and writing in English. In addition, it can enhance members’ English speaking and listening skills by encouraging them to play different roles in different dramatic contexts. As audience, setting up a drama club can enable students to learn English by appreciating a drama production. Members have to rehearse the drama by listening to others actors’ lines and by reading aloud their own lines. They can even create their script and interact with other members in English.

Handle Stardum

Probably the most important thing you can do as a successful actor is not let it get to your head. In spite of what the fans have told you, you are not the almighty. You are still just a flesh and blood person and as quickly as you became a star, that is how fast it can all come crashing down on you. So many stars who are big one day are gone the next and this dose of reality is too much for them to handle. As a result, their lives are never the same, many taking to drugs and booze.
